usr & usrcat

ultrafast shape recognition with/without CREDO atom types for ligand-based virtual screening

Jobs

New job

The input to usr is onefold:

  • A query ligand in SDF format. Totally 23,129,049 ligands collected from the All Clean subset of ZINC are to be screened.

The output from usr is twofold:

  • log.csv.gz: ZINC ID, USR score and USRCAT score of the top 10000 ligands geometrically similar to the query ligand.
  • ligands.sdf.gz: structures of the top 1000 ligands geometrically similar to the query ligand.

Tutorials

How to submit a new job

Open your query ligand in SDF format locally in the WebGL canvas. Here is a sample file.

The provided ligand file must contain exactly one query ligand.

Briefly describe your job. The job description is defaulted to your query ligand filename.

Fill in your email to receive notification upon job completion. Fill in any anonymous email should you not want to receive notification.

Submit your job.

Check your job status in the topmost table.

ZINC ligands

23,129,049 ligands are collected from versions 2012-04-26, 2013-01-10 and 2013-12-18 of the All Clean subset of ZINC. 93,903,333 low-energy conformers are generated by RDKit. We thank Professor John J. Irwin, the developer and maintainer of ZINC, for granting us permission to use ZINC with three conditions:

  • We shall provide links to http://zinc.docking.org/substance/zincid for top hits so that users can seek for the most current purchasing information at ZINC's official web site.
  • We shall limit the number of top hits for download to 1000 ligands from a single job.
  • We shall update our ligands when ZINC data is updated so that users can benefit from the most current ligand data.
